Lake Nona

Population

45,640

Walkability

75%

So you like to be in the center of it all? Geographically speaking, that would be altamonte springs—conveniently located in seminole county at the crossroads of interstate 4 and state road 436, this city is within minutes of any home or business in central florida.

A unique blend of upscale retail and office establishments, hotels, commercial space, restaurants, and housing in all price ranges, there truly is something for everyone in this area. Go wine tasting at Wine Styles, catch a movie at AMC Altamonte Mall 18, or grab some fine cuisine at the new Elephant Bar or classics like Seasons 52.

Whether you are looking for a single-family or multi-family homes on a lake or in a quiet community, keep in mind that Altamonte Springs is located in one of the more affluent areas of Central Florida. (Altamonte Springs boasts 10 percent more college graduates per capita than the Florida average.)

Work hard, play hard

Altamonte Residents like to work hard, play hard. Uptown Altamonte, the core of the Central Business District is living proof. This well-designed community has morphed from a suburban community to a diverse urban and economic center. Cranes Roost Park is at the heart of Uptown Altamonte surrounding Cranes Roost Lake and holds monthly events like Jazz Jams Uptown and Karaoke Madness, annual events like the “Red, Hot and Boom” mega fireworks display/concert and the Uptown Altamonte Car Show and almost-daily performances by the Choreographed Dancing Fountain Show. The lake is encircled by one mile of continuous walkways with benches and covered seating area, perfect for jogging, walks, picnics, and people-watching.
